Dr. Demis Hassabis

Demis Hassabis is a prominent figure in the A.I and computer science industry. His career started at a very young age and he has since then made massive scientific contributions; specifically in the field of A.I. Dr. Hassabis has spent most of his life dedicated to "solving intelligence", which involves understanding both human and artificial intelligence, and mapping the connections between the two. Of course, Hassabis does not work alone; one could argue that his greatest skill is putting brilliant minds together.
